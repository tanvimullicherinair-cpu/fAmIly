Build Components

Distance Sensor

Detects human presence in rooms. Enables energy-saving automation by identifying empty spaces.

Sound Sensor

Monitors noise levels to detect household tension. Triggers calming mode when needed.

LED Matrix

Visual feedback system displaying alerts, reminders, and ambient patterns for family communication.

Motor

Enables 360° rotation for room scanning and directional air quality monitoring.

Speaker

Delivers gentle audio reminders for meals, energy alerts, and calming sounds.

Air Quality Sensor

Monitors dust, fumes, and CO2 levels. Recommends ventilation for healthier indoor air.
